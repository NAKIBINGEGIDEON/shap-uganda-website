Our Programs

Transforming communities through innovative and impactful health programs.

Explore Our Focus Areas

We implement diverse programs to address critical health and development challenges in Uganda.

Maternal, Newborn, and Child Health

Improving health outcomes for mothers, newborns, and children through awareness, accessibility, and quality healthcare services.

Communicable and Non-Communicable Diseases

Focused interventions to prevent and control diseases like HIV/AIDS, malaria, diabetes, and hypertension.

Community Health Initiatives

Empowering communities to take charge of their health through capacity building, health promotion, and advocacy.

School Health Programs

Creating a healthier learning environment by addressing the health needs of students and staff in schools.

Environmental Health

Promoting sustainable environmental practices to improve sanitation, water quality, and reduce health risks.

Health System Strengthening

Enhancing the capacity of health facilities and systems for better service delivery and management.
